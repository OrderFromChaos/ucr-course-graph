There are currently three visualizations of the data:
  • Arc Diagram Visualization using protovis
    • Most readable of all visualizations so far
    • Red lines indicate OR dependencies (any one of the red lines) and blue lines indicated AND dependencies (all of the courses)
  • Vis.js Version
    • Zoom out to see all nodes
    • Dotted lines indicate OR dependencies (any one of the dotted lines) and solid lines indicated AND dependencies (all of the courses)
  • Sigma.js Version
    • Red lines indicate OR dependencies (any one of the red lines) and blue lines indicated AND dependencies (all of the courses)
